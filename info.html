Shaughnessy Restaurant

Picturesque garden vistas. Seasonal flora. Soothing ambiance and casual elegance. A relaxed yet sophisticated atmosphere.

These elements describe the Shaughnessy Restaurant at Vandusen Botanical Garden: One of Vancouver's best-kept secrets, and the intimate venue for our wedding.

The restaurant features a warm, wood-paneled foyer that leads you into a bright and open dining room. A heated patio overlooks the Bentall pool and seemingly endless garden backdrop.

A string quartet will serenade with beautiful music before and during our Ceremony, which will be held on the patio.

Cocktail hour will be held after the Ceremony: Let's celebrate, socialize and build up an appetite.

For dinner, a four-course seasonal West Coast menu will be served, along with a selection of wines that we have chosen for you. As the sun sets, savour your meal and bask in the twinkling lights floating in the darkness of the garden.

Our DJ, photographer, a fully-stocked bar and the two of us will keep the fun going throughout the evening. We look forward to seeing you, and let's make this a night to remember.
